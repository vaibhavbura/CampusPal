Industry Internship Summary 
 
The number of student internships has significantly increased over the last few years. Students 
completed the internships through Eduskills internship programme approved by AICTE.  
Internships done through EduSkills enables to fill the gap between academia and industry by 
providing industry-oriented curriculum access to the students by following ICT based 
approach that provides employment and entrepreneurship opportunities. Their domains 
include cloud, networking, AI ML, data analytics. Their lectures are delivered to students in 
virtual environment.
